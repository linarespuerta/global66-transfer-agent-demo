# Policy: Refunds

When a transfer fails or is cancelled before delivery, the sender is refunded.

## Eligibility

- Failed transfers (invalid account, name mismatch, bank rejection): **fully refunded**, including
  the Global66 fee, because the service was not delivered.
- Transfers cancelled by the customer before the funds are sent to the receiving bank: refunded
  minus any FX cost already incurred.
- Completed transfers (funds delivered to the receiving bank) are **not** refundable through this
  process — if the recipient hasn't seen them, that's a bank-posting question, not a refund.

## Amount

- For failed transfers, the customer receives back the **full original amount they paid**, in the
  original sending currency (e.g. the full COP amount, not the destination-currency amount).
- No FX loss is passed to the customer on a failed transfer.

## Timeline

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within **3–5 business days** of the failure
  being confirmed.
- If the customer paid by bank transfer, it may take an extra business day to appear depending on
  their bank.

## What to tell the customer

- State the refund amount (full original amount + fee) and the 3–5 business day window.
- Confirm it returns to the original payment method.
- Reassure them no FX loss applies on a failed transfer.
